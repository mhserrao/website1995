WELCOME TO THE NET UNIVERSE!!!

If you are reading this, then you are witnessing an invention that parallels, if not exceeds, the wheel; you are witnessing...THE INTERNET!!! In fact, with the Internet, you can make your own wheel if you wanted to! It is the finest new technology that connects all ends of the globe and features extraordinary functions that can be used right in the comfort of your own home! Welcome to the Net Universe!!

THE GLOBAL LIBRARY

You can now search for anything you need from the comfort of your home! The Internet is a global library of information! With Internet Explorer 1.0 installed on your Windows 95 system, you can search for any page that exists on the World Wide Web! But don’t feel overwhelmed! All information on the net is extremely navigable since you can narrow down your searches using keywords and search for keywords within any given page. You can use the search engine as a way to quickly navigate the web without remembering those long website names. Forget about your local library and take a step into this world-wide library!

IMAGES GALORE

Are you tired of your photography being limited to what you do and where you go? Well, with the Internet, you can access almost any picture within a matter of seconds! With the Internet, you don’t have to go to Jamaica to have a picture of a beautiful beach or to the Swiss Alps to have a picture of crazy snowboarder boarding down a blizzard-ridden mountain. Want to see what a lion looks like? Just head to yahoo.com and type in lion, hit enter and there you go! Lion pictures! You just type it into the browser’s search engine and the image will be in the palm of your hand!

CLASSIFIED ADS

Is your home a cluttered mess? Do you have a really old car that you want to sell? Well, with the Internet, these don’t have to be your problems anymore! With classified ads, you can sell anything you want to get rid of. You can also buy things from the Internet! If you want to clutter your home or own a really old car, there's always someone selling these items through the use of classified ads. Buy or sell, both options make the Internet an online shopping mall with a variety as large as your wallet!

MAPQUEST

Are you tired of getting lost on road trips using paper maps? Well, with the use of a website called MapQuest, you no longer have to plan any routes, get lost on your paper map nor get lost using your paper map! MapQuest is a website on the Internet that plans a route for you in a step-by-step manner, so that you don’t have to navigate the highways like a pioneer discovering North America! Type in your starting point and your destination and you’re there in 1-2-3!
